Good to Know
Is this real money?

No. GoalLine runs on Solana devnet and GL Credits have no real-world value — this is a hackathon demo, clearly labeled throughout the app.

Where does the data come from?

Live scores, odds, and match events are synced directly from TxLINE, TxODDS's real-time sports data feed, built for the World Cup Hackathon.

Do I need to know crypto to use this?

Not really — connecting a wallet takes one tap, and everything else works like a normal app from there.
